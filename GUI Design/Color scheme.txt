 Colour Scheme 
The colour palette adopts a warm, appetizing scheme that evokes culinary enthusiasm while maintaining professionalism. The primary colours include:

Primary: #D95F18 (Bold Orange)
Secondary: #401B06 (Dark Brown)
Neutral Base: #F2DCC2 (Warm Cream)
Accent 1: #F2B84B (Warm Yellow)
Accent 2: #B2BF4B (Olive Green)
